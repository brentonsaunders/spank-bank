
# Spank Bank

Man, I fucking HATE writing documentation.

A very simple repository for your NSFW links (instead of keeping them in an untitled Notepad document). Randomly selects a link for your enjoyment. Helps you keep track of how often you've BUSTED A NUT to each link.

The randomizer is balanced such that, as the number of links your Spank Bank increases, links that you've NUTTED to less often are promoted. That way you don't get bored from the same link over and over again -- it encourages variety.

Your Spank Bank is assigned a unique ID and stored encrypted on the server, so you can clear your browser history and still be able to recover your links at a later time or from another device. 

I.e., you can BEAT OFF on your laptop during the day, and then beat off AGAIN on your smartphone in bed at 2 a.m.
